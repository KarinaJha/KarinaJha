About Me

Iâ€™ve been in love with reading and writing ever since I can remember, and as a child I fell easily into the expansive realms of fiction and fantasy. The ability to create a narrative that had never existed anywhere before fascinated me. I aspired to inspire others the way the names on my bookshelves had inspired me time and time again.
Now, years later, I still harbor the deep-rooted desire to touch others with my words and open their eyes to ideas and perspectives they would never have seen otherwise. In school, my composition skills developed and I began to see writing as a platform for the exploration of societal issues and the expression of individual beliefs under an irregular light. Through creative writing, I learned to suggest alternative outlooks to controversial topics, shed light on the struggles of neglected groups, and introduce original ideas through atypical means. I believe that with the contribution of each unique voice, the world progresses in diversity and awareness, which is why I strive to improve my ability to communicate ideas effectively through my writing.
Clarity of communication, the use of technical composition tools, and the examination of complicated issues under new lights are the key elements of a truly powerful piece of writing.
